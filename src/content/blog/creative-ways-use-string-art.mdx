## 1. Minimalist Centerpieces

A smooth board with few nails and monochromatic thread can create clean shapes
(circles, arches) ideal for modern living rooms. Fewer points = faster assembly.

## 2. Stylized Linear Portraits

Use a black-and-white photo and convert the contrast into a thread path. The
result: an ethereal portrait that captures features without visual saturation.

## 3. Custom Typography

Initials, motivational words, or significant dates. Define the silhouette with
nails and fill with diagonal threads for depth.

## 4. Maps and Coordinates

Represent the outline of your city or country with nail contours and subtle
internal weave. Add coordinates in vinyl or paint for an elegant finish.

## 5. Collaborative Children's Art

Mark points on a board for children to pass the thread following numbers or
colors. Encourages concentration and motor skills without screens.

## 6. Modular Panels

Create several small pieces (20×20 cm) with related color palettes and compose a
dynamic grid you can rearrange by season.

## 7. Personalized Gifts

Combine name + icon (heart, star, hobby) in a compact piece. Add a touch of
metallic thread to highlight.

## 8. LED Lighting Integration

Place LED strips behind the perforated board. Diffuse light between threads
creates shadows and nocturnal relief.

## 9. Chromatic Series

Same design, different gradients. Explore harmonies (analogous, complementary)
and rotate according to mood.

## 10. Functional Art

Create a panel with integrated hooks for keys or accessories and border the
utility area with a decorative thread pattern that frames.

## Execution Tips

- Use mercerized cotton thread for stable tension.
- Pre-drill to prevent wood from splitting in dense pieces.
- Keep a printed guide under transparent tape to follow the design without
  deviation.
- Apply a matte sealer finish if the wood is very porous.
